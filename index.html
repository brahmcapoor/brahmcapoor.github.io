Hi! I'm Brahm, and I'm a member of Stanford University's class of 2019

I was born in India and moved to Singapore at the age of 4, and I lived there until I moved to the U.S. for college. I live my life by a very simple credo: if something is cool, it's worth doing. Luckily, I find almost everything at least a little cool, so I actively try and do as much as I can. For example, I'm majoring in Symbolic Systems, which is an interdisciplinary field formed at the nexus of Computer Science, Cognitive Psychology, Linguistics and Philosophy and I chose this major because I loved how I could get such varied perspectives on each of these subjects. In the future, I want to work in tech, but hopefully, I'll be able to do so in a way that allows me to leverage my interest in other fields as well.

As a result, I actively try to develop a knowledge base that is broad, as well as deep. I'm eager for any opportunity that allows me to do this, whether it be a research position or in industry.
